Where every ingredient tells a story. Family-owned artisan-crafted since 1980. No pre-made dishes, no shortcuts, just made-to-order, customizable dishes. Check out our new drinks in the beverages menu! 每一道食材都有故事——匠心1980年至今。拒絕科技與狠活預製菜與捷徑，只獻上即時鮮香。飲料菜單中有新飲料！
Authentic Szechuan & Cantonese flavors crafted by award-winning chefs trained at the Szechuan Higher Institute of Cuisine and Cantonese meals loved by the community for four decades. Stop by for dine-in, take-out or delivery! 地道川粵滋味由四川烹飪高等專科學校出身、屢獲大獎的名廚精心打造；粵式經典四十載深受街坊喜愛。
Free Wifi dining in! Lazy Susan revolving table for the perfect party. Bar with wine and beer (BYOB is O.K. too). 堂吃免費上網。旋轉圓桌享受快樂多人聚餐。可享用吧台的冰鎮啤酒等，也可以自帶酒飲。
